Our Firm

Cardinal Advisory Services was founded to bridge a gap between accounting and financial advisory services in the Caribbean. We deliver the highest quality through our emphasis on client services by exceeding expectations and adding value. We provide customized solutions to meet the needs of our clients whether you are a large financial institution or a sole proprietor. Cardinal Advisory is a growing consulting firm with vast experience in a range of industries - manufacturing, retail, government agencies, financial institutions and telecommunications

What We Do

Accounting
& Assurance

We provide solutions to businesses by ensuring effective financial systems and controls are in place to meet business and compliance requirements that are critical for success. We also offer assurance services for regulatory & statutory purposes

Tax Consulting

All financial decisions have tax implications. Let us help you anticipate problems and propose solutions that will enable you to achieve your goals, thereby effectively addressing your tax related issues.

Advisory

We help fill the gap in your accounting and financial needs by providing advice and support on a wide range of transactions or events. This includes but is not limited to adherence to new or revised International financial reporting standards (IFRS)

Training

We offer personal finance management training to businesses with a focus on equipping your staff to make better personal financial decisions. We also provide training in all the service areas we provide such as accounting, taxation and audit.
